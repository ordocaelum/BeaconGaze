# BeaconGaze — Design Brief (Phase 0)

Design read: For seekers of premium personalized spirituality; the emotional register is
awe + precision — an observatory built by a luxury house.

Concept spine: "The site is a telescope descending through your own sky." Every scroll
tick moves the visitor deeper through a 3D star field toward their natal wheel; their
birth data is the protagonist.

Delivery tier: spectacle (Three.js particle cosmos + GSAP ScrollTrigger scrub +
scroll-driven cinematic hero film + custom cursor glow + interactive natal engine).

Locked palette (user-mandated brand colors, overriding default bans):
- Liquid dark matter  #050310  (page void)
- Deep nebula violet  #8b6cf0 / #2a1b52
- Solar flare orange  #ff8a3c
- Pulsar blue         #4fc3ff
- Starlight silver    #e9e6f4
Defense: the five iridescent states the client specified; every gradient interpolates
only inside this family.

Locked type: Cormorant Garamond 300 (massive display serif — high-fashion mandate from
the brief) + Space Grotesk (HUD/labels/body). Serif justified by explicit
"elegant, high-fashion typography" brand requirement.

Tier-1 technique: scroll-scrubbed cinematic film hero (scroll drives video time) layered
over an interactive Three.js constellation field with camera dolly mapped to scroll.
Enacts the spine literally: the wheel of the sky plays only as the visitor descends.

Section plan (8, ≥4 layout families, no consecutive repeats):
1. Cosmic Nexus — full-bleed cinematic scrub hero
2. The Blueprint — split editorial + live natal-wheel instrument (interactive input)
3. Orbital Alignments — horizontal metric band over second film plate
4. Quantum Intellect — offset asymmetric feature grid, HUD glass cards
5. The Inner Journey — centered narrative chapter, stacked reveals
6. Aura & Spectrum — full-bleed personal color matrix (generated from chart)
7. Celestial Specifications — data table dossier, monospace precision
8. Claim Your Destiny — cathedral CTA, dual bespoke CTAs

Asset plan: hero film (Cinema Studio Video, generating), two celestial sequences
(pending credits), procedural Three.js cosmos + SVG natal instrument as always-on
native layers, monogram + favicon inline SVG, OG card.

CTA inventory: "Begin the Descent" (hero — magnetic ring, orbiting particle),
"Cast My Chart" (blueprint — filament underline draw), "Reserve a Reading"
(final — eclipse fill sweep), "Acquire Ritual Kit" (final — silver outline orbit).
